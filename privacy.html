1Who we are

CaleSync is a web application built and maintained by Chirag Aggarwal (@chiiragaggarwal). The app is hosted on GitHub Pages at https://chiiragaggarwal.github.io/CaleSync.

CaleSync helps users set reminder notifications on their Google Calendar events so they never miss a meeting. The app runs entirely in your browser — there is no backend server, no database, and no data storage of any kind on our end.

2What data we access

When you sign in with Google, CaleSync requests the following permissions:

Google Calendar data

  • Read access: We read your upcoming calendar events for the next 30 days including event titles, start times, and RSVP status
  • Write access: We modify the reminder settings on individual events to add popup notifications at your chosen interval

Google profile data

  • Your name and profile photo (displayed in the app header)
  • Your email address (displayed as the connected account)

CaleSync only accesses your primary calendar. We do not access other calendars, Gmail, Google Drive, or any other Google services.

3How we use your data

Your data is used solely to provide the core functionality of CaleSync:

  • Fetching your upcoming events to display them in the Events tab
  • Patching reminder settings on each event via the Google Calendar API
  • Displaying your name and photo in the app navigation

Your OAuth access token is stored in sessionStorage — a temporary browser storage that is automatically cleared when you close the browser tab. It is never sent to any server other than Google's own APIs.

4What we do NOT do

We want to be explicitly clear about what CaleSync does not do:

  • ❌ We do not store your calendar data on any server
  • ❌ We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • ❌ We do not use your data for advertising or analytics
  • ❌ We do not retain your OAuth token after your session ends
  • ❌ We do not access your emails, contacts, or other Google services
  • ❌ We do not create accounts or user profiles
  • ❌ We do not track your usage or behaviour

5Data storage

CaleSync has no backend server and no database. The app is a static HTML file hosted on GitHub Pages.

The only data stored locally on your device is:

  • sessionStorage: Your OAuth access token and basic profile info (name, email, photo URL). This is cleared automatically when you close the browser tab or click Sign out.

No data from your Google account is ever transmitted to, stored on, or processed by any server other than Google's own servers.

6Third-party services

CaleSync uses the following third-party services:

Google Identity Services

Used for authentication. When you sign in, you interact directly with Google's OAuth system. Google's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service apply to this interaction.

Google Calendar API

Used to read events and set reminders. All API calls are made directly from your browser to Google's servers using your access token.

Google Fonts

Used to load the Inter typeface. Google may log font requests per their Privacy Policy.

GitHub Pages

Used to host the app. GitHub may collect standard web server logs (IP address, browser type) per their Privacy Statement.

7Your rights

Since CaleSync does not store your data, there is nothing for us to delete or provide. However, you have full control over your data:

  • Revoke access anytime: Go to myaccount.google.com/permissions and remove CaleSync
  • Sign out: Clicking Sign out in the app clears your session immediately
  • Remove reminders: You can manually remove any reminder set by CaleSync directly in Google Calendar

8Children's privacy

CaleSync is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has used this app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Continued use of CaleSync after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
